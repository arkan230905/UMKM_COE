Depreciation Formula Verification

This page verifies the correct implementation of depreciation methods.

Test Asset Data

  • Cost: Rp 1,000,000
  • Residual Value: Rp 100,000
  • Useful Life: 5 years
  • Acquisition Date: January 1, 2024
  • Current Date: April 20, 2026 (28 months elapsed)

1. Straight Line Method

Formula: (Cost - Residual) ÷ (Life × 12)

Calculation: (1,000,000 - 100,000) ÷ (5 × 12) = 900,000 ÷ 60 = 15,000

Monthly Depreciation: Rp 15,000 (constant)

Accumulated (28 months): 15,000 × 28 = Rp 420,000

Current Book Value: 1,000,000 - 420,000 = Rp 580,000

2. Double Declining Balance Method

Formula: Book Value × (2 ÷ Life) ÷ 12

Annual Rate: 2 ÷ 5 = 40%

Monthly Rate: 40% ÷ 12 = 3.333%
